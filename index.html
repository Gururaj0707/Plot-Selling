Legal Checklist for Buyers

Our comprehensive guide to ensure a smooth buying process.
Title Deed: Verify the seller’s ownership and ensure no disputes.
Encumbrance Certificate: Confirm no outstanding loans or legal claims.
Approved Layout: Ensure the plot is part of an authorized layout.
Land Use Clearance: Check government permissions for intended use.
Tax Receipts: Verify all property taxes are paid up to date.
NOCs: Obtain required No Objection Certificates.
